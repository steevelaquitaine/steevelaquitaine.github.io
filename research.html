Steeve Laquitaine, PhD

Postdoc at Sorbonne Universite, Paris Vision Institute & Affiliate at Stanford University , ex-EPFL

I study efficient inference & decision-making in biological & artificial neural networks. I use mathematical theory, computational models to link large-scale neural recordings and psychophysics.
